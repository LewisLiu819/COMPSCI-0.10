# Week 13: Digital Citizenship and Global Connections

**Unit:** Computers and Society (Weeks 12-13)
**Duration:** 2 lessons, 50 minutes each

## Week Overview

Week 13 concludes Unit 5 by focusing on responsible technology use and global awareness. Students learn essential digital citizenship skills and explore how technology connects people around the world, preparing them to be thoughtful and responsible digital citizens.

## Learning Goals

By the end of Week 13, students will be able to:
- Understand basic principles of online safety and digital citizenship
- Demonstrate responsible behavior when using technology
- Recognize how technology connects people globally
- Appreciate cultural diversity through digital connections

## Lessons This Week

### [Lesson 13.1: Digital Citizenship and Online Safety](lesson13.1.md)
**Format:** Safety Education + Responsible Technology Use

Students learn fundamental digital citizenship concepts including online safety, password security, and responsible digital communication.

**Key Activities:**
- Digital footprint investigation and awareness
- Password security workshop and practice
- Online interaction scenarios and response practice
- Digital citizenship pledge creation

**Vocabulary:** Digital citizenship, Digital footprint, Password security, Privacy, Cyberbullying

---

### [Lesson 13.2: Technology for Global Connections](lesson13.2.md)
**Format:** Cultural Exploration + Global Awareness

Students explore how technology enables global communication and cultural exchange, developing appreciation for diversity and understanding of global citizenship.

**Key Activities:**
- Global technology exploration and access awareness
- Virtual cultural exchange opportunities
- Global problem-solving discussions
- World connections reflection and goal setting

**Vocabulary:** Global connections, Cultural exchange, International collaboration, Digital divide, Global citizenship

## Assessment Highlights

- **Formative:** Digital citizenship understanding, cultural awareness demonstration
- **Performance:** Scenario responses and global communication skills
- **Portfolio:** Digital citizenship pledge and global awareness reflections

## Cross-Curricular Connections

- **Social Studies:** Global awareness, cultural diversity, and citizenship education
- **Character Education:** Responsibility, empathy, and ethical behavior
- **Language Arts:** Communication skills and cultural appreciation
- **Geography:** Global connections and international understanding

## Digital Citizenship Focus

- Online safety and security awareness
- Responsible digital communication and behavior
- Understanding of digital footprints and privacy
- Respectful interaction in digital spaces

## Global Awareness and Cultural Understanding

- Recognition of worldwide technology access variations
- Appreciation for cultural diversity and similarities
- Understanding of global challenges and opportunities
- Development of global citizenship mindset

- Resources for continuing safety conversations at home
- Guidelines for family technology use agreements
- Support for ongoing digital responsibility development

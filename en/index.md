## Course Overview

This course consists of 15 lessons, each lasting 60 minutes, with 20-30 minutes dedicated to introducing new concepts and the remaining time for hands-on practice and discussion. The course aims to help students understand fundamental computer science and programming concepts through engaging activities and projects, and learn to apply these concepts to real-world problems.

# Elementary Computer Science: "Coding Adventures"
*Adapted from UC Berkeley CS10 for Grades 4-6*

## Course Overview
**Duration:** 16 weeks (32 lessons, 2 sessions per week)
**Class Time:** 50 minutes per session
**Target Age:** Grades 4-6 (Ages 9-12)
**Class Size:** 15-20 students
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Course Structure

### [Unit 1: What is Computer Science?](week01/) (Weeks 1-2)
- [Week 1](week01/) - Introduction to computers and digital world
- [Week 2](week02/) - Thinking like a computer and algorithms

### [Unit 2: Building Blocks of Programming](week03/) (Weeks 3-5)
- [Week 3](week03/) - Introduction to Scratch Jr. and basic programming
- [Week 4](week04/) - Conditionals and interactive programming
- [Week 5](week05/) - Loops and patterns

### [Unit 3: Problem Solving and Logic](week06/) (Weeks 6-8)
- [Week 6](week06/) - Boolean logic and decision making
- [Week 7](week07/) - Debugging and pair programming
- [Week 8](week08/) - Data organization and digital projects

### [Unit 4: Creative Computing](week09/) (Weeks 9-11)
- [Week 9](week09/) - Digital storytelling and character development
- [Week 10](week10/) - Game design and interactive experiences
- [Week 11](week11/) - Digital art and music programming

### [Unit 5: Computers and Society](week12/) (Weeks 12-13)
- [Week 12](week12/) - Technology careers and social impact
- [Week 13](week13/) - Digital citizenship and global connections

### [Final Projects and Assessment](week14/) (Weeks 14-16)
- [Week 14](week14/) - Capstone project planning and development
- [Week 15](week15/) - Project completion and presentations
- [Week 16](week16/) - Showcase event and course reflection

# Lesson 1.1: Welcome to the Digital World

**Let's get started on our computer science adventure!** In this first lesson, we're putting on our detective hats to uncover the secret world of computers all around us.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of our adventure today, you'll be able to:
- Explain what a computer is in your own words.
- Find at least 5 different computers hiding in plain sight.
- Share one cool way that computers help us every day.
- Show that you know how to be a responsible user of our classroom tech.

## Our Mission for Today

### Warm-Up Circle (10 minutes)
- Let's get to know each other!
- We'll brainstorm all the things that pop into your head when you hear the word "computer."
- We'll go over the rules for using our cool classroom gadgets.

### Main Adventure: Computer Detective Hunt! (25 minutes)
- With a partner, you'll grab a special "detective clipboard."
- Your mission: explore the classroom and school to find and jot down all the different computers you see.
- Look for three types of clues: traditional computers (like laptops), smart devices (like phones), and hidden "embedded" computers (like in a microwave!).

### Detective Debrief (10 minutes)
- Let's share all the amazing discoveries you made!
- We'll create a giant class chart to display all the computers we found.
- Get a sneak peek at our next exciting lesson.

### Tech Time (5 minutes)
- A quick introduction to the tablets and computers we'll be using.
- We'll practice how to handle them carefully and find our way around the screen.

## Your Detective Gear
- Detective clipboards with a sheet to record your findings
- Pencils and crayons for drawing and writing
- A big sheet of paper for our class computer list
- The classroom gadgets we'll be exploring
- Picture cards to help us identify different computers

---

## How We'll Know You're a Super Detective
- We'll see how you work with your partner and how many cool computers you can find.
- **Your Mission Report:** You'll draw one computer you found and write one sentence about how it helps people. This will be your "exit ticket" to show what you've learned!

## Fun for Everyone
- **Want a bigger challenge?** Try to find the most surprising or unusual computer in the school.
- **Need a little help?** We have picture cards to give you clues, and you can team up with a partner.

## Tech Words to Know
- **Computer:** A machine that follows instructions to help people solve problems or do tasks.
- **Device:** A special tool made for a specific job, like a tablet or a camera.
- **Technology:** All the cool tools and machines people have invented to make life easier.
- **Smart:** When a device is "smart," it means it can connect to the internet and make some decisions on its own, like a smartphone!

## Real-World Applications
- **Community connections:** Libraries, stores, restaurants with computer systems
- **Transportation:** Traffic lights, GPS systems, automatic doors
- **Healthcare:** Digital thermometers, appointment systems
- **Entertainment:** Gaming systems, digital movie theaters

# Lesson 11.2: Music Composition with Code

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Format:** Music Creation + Programming Integration
**Unit:** Creative Computing (Week 9-11)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Create original musical compositions using programming
- Understand basic musical concepts through coding
- Combine multiple musical elements in programmed compositions
- Appreciate the connection between mathematics, music, and programming

## Lesson Structure

### Music and Programming Connection (10 minutes)
- Explore how music and programming share similar concepts
- Discuss rhythm, patterns, and sequences in both music and code
- Listen to examples of computer-generated music
- Plan musical programming projects

### Digital Music Composition (30 minutes)
- Create original musical pieces using sound blocks and loops
- Experiment with rhythm, melody, and harmony
- Layer multiple musical elements for complex compositions
- Add visual elements that respond to or complement the music

### Composition Sharing Concert (10 minutes)
- Students perform their musical compositions for the class
- Practice active listening and constructive feedback
- Appreciate variety in musical styles and programming approaches
- Celebrate creativity and musical expression

## Materials Needed
- Devices with Scratch Jr. and sound capabilities
- Headphones for individual composition work
- Simple musical notation examples for reference
- Recording capability for saving compositions
- Concert performance setup

## Assessment

### Formative
- Understanding of musical concepts through programming
- Creativity and originality in musical composition
- Successful use of programming tools for music creation
- Quality of musical and visual integration

## Music Integration
- Basic music theory: rhythm, melody, harmony
- Composition techniques and musical structure
- Cultural music exploration and appreciation
- Performance skills and audience presentation

## Mathematical Connections
- Pattern recognition and creation
- Timing and mathematical relationships in music
- Frequency and pitch concepts
- Symmetry and structure in musical composition

## Extension Opportunities
- Create soundtracks for digital stories or games
- Program musical instruments or interactive installations
- Collaborate with music teachers on cross-curricular projects
- Explore careers in music technology and audio engineering

## Vocabulary Introduced/Reinforced
- Composition
- Rhythm
- Melody
- Harmony
- Music technology

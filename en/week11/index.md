# Week 11: Digital Art and Music Programming

**Unit:** Creative Computing (Weeks 9-11)
**Duration:** 2 lessons, 50 minutes each

## Week Overview

Week 11 concludes Unit 4 by exploring the intersection of technology and creative arts. Students create digital artwork and musical compositions using programming, learning how technology can enhance artistic expression and discovering new forms of creative media.

## Learning Goals

By the end of Week 11, students will be able to:
- Create digital artwork using programming tools
- Understand how technology enhances artistic expression
- Compose original music using programming concepts
- Combine visual and audio elements in integrated projects

## Lessons This Week

### [Lesson 11.1: Digital Art Gallery Programming](lesson11.1.md)
**Format:** Art Creation + Programming Integration

Students create interactive digital artwork that combines programming logic with artistic expression, exploring how technology opens new possibilities for creative expression.

**Key Activities:**
- Digital art exploration and inspiration
- Interactive art creation with programming elements
- Virtual gallery setup and organization
- Art appreciation and critique

**Vocabulary:** Digital art, Interactive art, Artist statement, Creative medium, Visual design

---

### [Lesson 11.2: Music Composition with Code](lesson11.2.md)
**Format:** Music Creation + Programming Integration

Students compose original musical pieces using programming tools, learning about the mathematical relationships in music and creating multimedia compositions.

**Key Activities:**
- Music and programming connection exploration
- Digital music composition with loops and sequences
- Integration of visual elements with musical compositions
- Concert performance and sharing

**Vocabulary:** Composition, Rhythm, Melody, Harmony, Music technology

## Assessment Highlights

- **Formative:** Creativity in digital art creation, understanding of music-programming connections
- **Performance:** Art gallery presentation and musical composition performance
- **Portfolio:** Digital artworks and musical compositions for creative portfolio

## Cross-Curricular Connections

- **Visual Arts:** Elements and principles of design, art history, creative expression
- **Music:** Rhythm, melody, harmony, and musical composition techniques
- **Mathematics:** Patterns, symmetry, and mathematical relationships in art and music
- **Cultural Studies:** Art and music from different cultures and traditions

## Technology as Creative Medium

- Understanding of digital tools for artistic creation
- Exploration of new possibilities in creative expression
- Integration of traditional art concepts with modern technology
- Preparation for future digital art and design opportunities

## Creative Expression Focus

- Personal artistic voice and creative choices
- Experimentation with new forms of creative media
- Integration of multiple artistic elements
- Appreciation for diverse forms of artistic expression

## Materials Needed

- Devices with Scratch Jr. and multimedia capabilities
- Digital art examples for inspiration
- Simple musical instruments or sound-making tools
- Art planning and reflection worksheets
- Gallery and concert presentation setup

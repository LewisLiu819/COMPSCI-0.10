# Lesson 16.2: Showcase Event and Course Reflection

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Format:** Public Showcase + Learning Reflection
**Unit:** Final Projects and Assessment (Week 14-16)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Successfully present their learning to families and community members
- Reflect on their learning journey and growth throughout the course
- Articulate the value and importance of computer science education
- Set goals for continued learning and exploration

## Lesson Structure

### Community Showcase Event (35 minutes)
- Students present projects and learning to invited families and community
- Demonstrate programming skills and explain technical concepts
- Share learning journey and growth experiences
- Receive recognition and celebration from audience

### Course Reflection and Goal Setting (12 minutes)
- Reflect on learning journey from beginning to end of course
- Identify key skills, concepts, and confidence gained
- Set goals for continued computer science learning and exploration
- Appreciate growth and achievement in themselves and classmates

### Celebration and Recognition (3 minutes)
- Celebrate individual and class achievements
- Recognize effort, creativity, persistence, and growth
- Thank families, community members, and each other
- Look forward to continued learning and exploration

## Materials Needed
- Showcase event setup with student project stations
- Portfolio and documentation displays
- Recognition certificates and celebration materials
- Reflection and goal-setting materials
- Thank you and appreciation materials

## Assessment

### Summative
- Quality of showcase presentation to community audience
- Demonstration of course learning objectives mastery
- Reflection on learning journey and growth
- Goal setting for continued learning and exploration

### Portfolio Assessment
Complete course portfolio demonstrating learning journey, skill development, and achievement of all course objectives.

## Community Connection and Recognition

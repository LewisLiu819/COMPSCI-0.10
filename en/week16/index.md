# Week 16: Showcase Event and Course Reflection

**Unit:** Final Projects and Assessment (Weeks 14-16)
**Duration:** 2 lessons, 50 minutes each

## Week Overview

Week 16 concludes the course with a celebration of learning and growth. Students prepare for a community showcase event, reflect on their learning journey, and set goals for continued exploration in computer science and technology.

## Learning Goals

By the end of Week 16, students will be able to:
- Present their learning to families and community members
- Reflect comprehensively on their learning journey and growth
- Articulate the value and importance of computer science education
- Set meaningful goals for continued learning and exploration

## Lessons This Week

### [Lesson 16.1: Showcase Event Preparation](lesson16.1.md)
**Format:** Event Planning + Final Preparations

Students prepare for a public showcase of their learning, organizing displays, practicing presentations, and celebrating their achievements with the broader community.

**Key Activities:**
- Showcase event planning and logistics
- Presentation practice for diverse audiences
- Professional display and documentation preparation
- Celebration and recognition preparation

**Vocabulary:** Showcase event, Professional presentation, Community engagement, Learning documentation, Achievement celebration

---

### [Lesson 16.2: Showcase Event and Course Reflection](lesson16.2.md)
**Format:** Public Showcase + Learning Reflection

Students present their work to families and community members, reflect on their complete learning journey, and celebrate their growth and achievements.

**Key Activities:**
- Community showcase event with family presentations
- Comprehensive course reflection and goal setting
- Learning journey appreciation and recognition
- Future learning pathway exploration

**Vocabulary:** Community presentation, Learning journey, Growth reflection, Goal setting, Future pathways

## Assessment Highlights

- **Performance:** Quality of showcase presentation to community audience
- **Reflection:** Comprehensive learning journey analysis and growth recognition
- **Goal Setting:** Meaningful future learning and exploration objectives
- **Portfolio:** Complete course portfolio demonstrating learning and achievement

## Community Engagement and Recognition

- Successful sharing of learning with broader community
- Recognition of student achievement and growth
- Building support for computer science education
- Inspiring continued learning and exploration

## Learning Journey Reflection

- Recognition of individual growth and achievement
- Appreciation for learning process and challenges overcome
- Understanding of computer science concepts and skills gained
- Confidence in continued learning and exploration

## Future Learning Pathways

- Connection to middle school computer science opportunities
- Identification of summer programs and continued learning options
- Goal setting for skill development and exploration
- Building foundation for lifelong learning in technology

## Materials Needed

- Showcase event setup with student project stations
- Portfolio and documentation displays
- Recognition certificates and celebration materials
- Family and community invitation materials
- Reflection and goal-setting resources

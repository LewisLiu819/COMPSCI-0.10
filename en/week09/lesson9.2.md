# Lesson 9.2: Character Development and Voice Acting

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Format:** Creative Arts + Advanced Programming
**Unit:** Creative Computing (Week 9-11)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Design unique digital characters with distinct personalities
- Add voice recording to bring characters to life
- Create character interactions and dialogue
- Demonstrate understanding of character development principles

## Lesson Structure

### Character Design Workshop (15 minutes)
- Analyze memorable characters from books, movies, and games
- Identify what makes characters unique and interesting
- Design original characters with specific personality traits
- Plan character appearance, voice, and behavior

### Voice Acting and Recording (20 minutes)
- Practice different character voices and speaking styles
- Record character dialogue and sound effects
- Learn basic voice acting techniques (pace, emotion, clarity)
- Add recorded voices to digital characters

### Character Interaction Programming (12 minutes)
- Program conversations between multiple characters
- Create character-specific behaviors and movements
- Practice timing dialogue with character actions
- Test character interactions for clarity and entertainment

### Character Showcase (3 minutes)
- Brief presentations of unique characters
- Appreciation for creativity and variety
- Feedback on character development and voice work

## Materials Needed
- Character design worksheets
- Recording capability (tablets with microphones or simple recording devices)
- Devices with Scratch Jr.
- Character personality trait cards for inspiration
- Headphones for private listening during recording

## Assessment

### Formative
- Creativity and thoughtfulness in character design
- Quality of voice acting and recording
- Successful integration of audio with programming
- Character development depth and personality

## Arts Integration
- Drama and theater skills through voice acting
- Visual arts through character design
- Music and sound through audio recording
- Creative writing through dialogue development

## Performance and Presentation Skills
- Public speaking and voice projection
- Character interpretation and embodiment
- Audience awareness and engagement
- Confidence building through creative expression

## Technology Skills
- Audio recording and editing basics
- File management for multimedia projects
- Integration of multiple media types
- Quality control and testing for user experience

## Extension Opportunities
- Create character-based game shows or quiz programs
- Develop ongoing character series with multiple episodes
- Collaborate with other classes to create shared character universes
- Create character-based educational content for younger students

## Vocabulary Introduced/Reinforced
- Character development
- Voice acting
- Dialogue
- Personality traits
- Audio recording
